## Operating principles

### Evidence before hype

Claims should trace to verified facts, documented assumptions, working examples, or clearly labeled recommendations.

### Human authority where consequences matter

AI can analyze, organize, recommend, and execute authorized actions. Human authority remains explicit for consequential decisions.

### Operational systems—not decorative dashboards

A useful system should complete work, verify the result, preserve evidence, and update the source of truth.

### Public proof without private exposure

Our public repositories provide reusable tools, sanitized patterns, and technical examples without exposing confidential implementation details, private operating records, or client information.
